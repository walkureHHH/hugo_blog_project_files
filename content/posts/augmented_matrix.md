How to write augmented matrix by Latex.

<!--more-->

Example:

```latex
\left [
\begin{array}{c|c}
\begin{matrix}
3.333&15920&10.333\\
2.222&16.710&9.6120\\
-1.561&5.179&-1.686
\end{matrix}
&
\begin{matrix}
7953\\0.965\\2.714
\end{matrix}
\end{array}
\right ]
```
